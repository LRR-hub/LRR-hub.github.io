ScreenCapturer Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 17, 2025

Introduction

ScreenCapturer Pro is a local screenshot tool application developed by ScreenCapturer Development Team. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data remains secure.

Data Collection

ScreenCapturer Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users. All screenshots taken with the application are saved locally on your device and are never shared with third parties or our servers.

Permissions

The application only requests screen recording permissions, which are necessary to capture screenshots. These permissions are only active while using the application and are not used for any other purpose.

Third-Party Services

ScreenCapturer Pro does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. Your data never leaves your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
